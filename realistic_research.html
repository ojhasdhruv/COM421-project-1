Realistic Research Module Overview

This lesson is designed for students beginning research in their first semester of the TCID, IA, or Ph.D. in Technical Communication. It provides strategies to develop, improve, or refresh your research skills while guiding you through accessing a variety of resources.

Content Areas

  • Getting Started
  • Research Timelines
  • Requesting Resources
  • Local Libraries
  • Finding Resources on the Web

Getting Started

Learn how to access IIT library services and use myIIT to reach the library's resources. If you are new and haven't obtained an IIT Hawk Card, follow the instructions on the Hawk Card Homepage.

Research Timelines

When planning your research, consider the time needed to obtain, read, and integrate a resource into your project. Use the following formula as a guideline:

Time to obtain resource + Time to read resource + Time to integrate resource < Remaining time before deadline.

Requesting Resources

Sometimes, direct downloads aren't available. In these cases, request articles or books through interlibrary loan or myILL to ensure you have the resources you need.
